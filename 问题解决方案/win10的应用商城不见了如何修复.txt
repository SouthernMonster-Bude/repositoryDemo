进入输入powershell应用窗口，命令Get-AppxPackage -allusers | Select Name, PackageFullName后回车

找到Microsoft.WindowsStore(应用商店)这个应用，然后复制右侧对应的包名称 Microsoft.WindowsStore_xxxx.xxx.xxxx.xx_x64__8wekyb3d8bbwe

Add-appxpackage -register "C:\Program Files\WindowsApps\Microsoft.WindowsStore_xxxx.xxx.xxxx.xx_x64__8wekyb3d8bbwe\appxmanifest.xml" -disabledevelopmentmode
